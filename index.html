Communication, Networking, & Job Searching Made Simple

all on a platform dedicated to dentistry

The Problem: Dentistry lacks a

trusted online resource to network with peers

A lack of investment and innovation in dentistry has left everyone with the following painpoints: 1) no single communication tool to handle groups, flows of information, and connections, 2) no effective place to hire quality staff and find open positions, and 3) no efficient platform to discover and network with dental companies. Dentistry lacks an powerful integrated solution.

An Integrated Solution,

The Dental Files

TDF is a professional and social app for the dental industry. More effective than current social platforms like Facebook or Reddit, features are built with dentistry in mind. TDF takes elements of existing social tools and integrates them into one solution to finally bring dental students, dentists, dental professionals, and dental companies onto one collorative space.

Manage Groups

Create and manage all your dental groups - public or private - in a single space dedicated to dentistry.

Collaborate

Join group conversations, share your knowledge to various communities, and learn from your peers.

Network

Grow your dental network with access to professionals on a national scale, and/or strengthen your existing relationships.

Hire & Find Jobs

Get to know who your candidates are prior to scheduling the interview, and find quality dental jobs based on your personal preferences.

The Key to success is

establishing the Network

Our business model relies on building a network for dental students, professionals, and dentists. Our strategy is to first target dental students because they are technologically saavy, desperate for jobs, and represent the next generation of dentists. We will grow our user base by running online AMAs (ask me anythings) where students can ask questions to prominent dentists looking to share their knowledge. Additionally, the focus will be on building valuable product features to create a viral product that markets itself within dentistry to onboard dentists and beyond.
